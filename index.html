the garden

the garden is rich with harvest
even in the winter months
the root vegetables holding
their sweetness under the snow.

my mother’s hands could always
dig in the earth and pull out
something good. her touch
could make a fallen bulb rise
again.

and she would stand in her small space
fleshy and fragrant
a woman of color in a garden
of green thumbs.

the garden is rich with harvest
she is the garden.

— Lucille Clifton
